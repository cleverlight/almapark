Maxine runs the on-site catering business. Whether you need a quick coffee in between meetings or a fully-catered breakfast, lunch and afternoon tea package to punctuate your event, her team can ensure that you're kept well nourished.

The site has a Cafe open from 8am to 3pm, or later to cater evening events.

You can order freshly ground coffee, tea or soft drinks or have them delivered to your meeting room.

Maxine serves a wide range of delicious food including hot breakfast rolls, light meals, homemade cakes, sandwiches and is delighted to come up with a menu to meet your special requirements.
