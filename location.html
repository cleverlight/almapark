Alma Park, Woodway Lane, Claybrooke Parva, Lutterworth, Leicester, LE17 5BH.

Alma Park, at the heart of the Midlands Motorway 'Golden' triangle of the M1, M6 and M69 and close to major rail and air links, will provide 20,000 sq ft of unique office space.

Many units are now completed, with a number of businesses already in situ and the remaining units now close to completion. This web site provides details of the Alma Park development, including plans, photographs and comprehensive information.

Alma Park has been located on the Leicestershire / Warwickshire borders, at the crossroads of the ancient Roman roads Fosse Way and Watling Street, for over 300 years.
