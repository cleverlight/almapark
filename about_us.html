Alma Park sits just 1 mile away from the Roman centre of England. The intersection of the A5 (Watling Street) and B4455 (Fosse way), is the site of a large Roman settlement.

The fields surrounding Alma House frequently turn up ancient pottery, a collection of which will be available for viewing onsite.
