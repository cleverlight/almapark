Alma Park provides conference facilities, meeting rooms and office space to businesses based in the rural heart of England.

Environmental focus

Heating for all the offices and meeting rooms is supplied by an eco-friendly wood-chip burner. Some 20 acres of poplar trees are being planted to provide a renewable fuel source.

Alma Park has been located on the Leicestershire / Warwickshire borders, at the crossroads of the ancient Roman roads Fosse Way and Watling Street, for over 300 years.
